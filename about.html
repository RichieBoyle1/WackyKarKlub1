About Wacky Kar Klub

Wacky Kar Klub is a Car loving community for vintage and classic car enthusiasts. Our mission is to bring together car lovers across The North of Ireland and showcase a variety of unique vehicles.

Founded in 2010, we have grown to include over 500 registered members and host regular rallies, shows, and meetups. Members can enjoy exclusive events and access specialist services for their vehicles.

Our Values

  • Passion for vintage and classic cars
  • Community engagement and support
  • Sharing knowledge and expertise
  • Promoting safe and responsible motoring
